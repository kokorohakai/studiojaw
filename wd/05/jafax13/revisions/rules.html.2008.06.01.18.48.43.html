General Rules for JAFAX Attendees.
- Weapons must be left in your vehicle. This includes any props that can be used as a weapon at the discretion of JAFAX staff.
     > - No loose chain over a foot long.
- No shirt, no shoes, no service! Because we are on a university, this is not our choice.
- Be courteous! Don't do anything to someone else that you wouldn't want done to you.
- No obscene or solicitation signs.
     > This include "Free hugs" or other offers for physical contact and receiving of money. This does not include signs that are used for cosplay.
