Where is Grand Valley State University?

GVSU is located directly West of Grand Rapids, in Michigan. Grand Valley is on the corner of Lake Michigan Drive and 48th Ave.

The address is:
1 Campus Drive
Allendale, MI 49401

Where on campus is JAFAX?

JAFAX will be held in Loutit, Padnos, Manitou, and Mackinac halls, and the Kirkhoff Center.

  • The Main room, sub and dub rooms, guests, and vendors will be in Loutit (44 on the map)
  • Con Ops South, the tabletop gaming room, and the panel room will be in Padnos at the top of the main staircase (55 on the map, accessable directly from Loutit)
  • The video game room, adult room, theme room, and Iron Man contest will be in lower level Manitou (47 on the map)
  • Most of the showing rooms, Artist's Alley, and Con Ops North will be in Mackinac (46 on the map)
  • The Costume Contest and Closing Ceremonies will be held in the Kirkhoff Center (36 on the map)
