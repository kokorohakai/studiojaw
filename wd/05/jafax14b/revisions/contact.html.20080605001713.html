Contacting us

By e-mail:
General inqueries can be sent to info@jafax.org
If you would like to help out at JAFAX, contact volunteer@jafax.org or see our volunteer page.
If you would like to sponsor JAFAX, contact sponsors@jafax.org
Vendors, please contact vendors@jafax.org or see our vendors page
Artists who would like to show their work, please contact artistalley@jafax.org

Also, our Forums are a great place to contact us, find information, and ask questions
