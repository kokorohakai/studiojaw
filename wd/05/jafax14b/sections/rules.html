General Rules for JAFAX Attendees

  1. Parents, you are responsible for your kids! The family-friendly room is full of programming just for them, but it is not a daycare.
  2. Weapons must be left in your vehicle. This includes any props that can be used as a weapon at the discretion of JAFAX staff. Due to being held on a University campus, we are bound by a pretty strict weapons policy, there are a lot of props that you could carry around at other conventions that you can not carry around here. Weapons include:
    1. Anything sharp and over 3 inches long
    2. Anything that could be used as a club; solid objects over a foot long
    3. Loose chain over a foot long
  3. No shirt, no shoes, no entrance!
  4. Be courteous! Don't do anything to someone else that you wouldn't want done to you.
  5. No solicitiation signs. This includes any signs offering physical contact or anything asking for money. This does not include signs that are part of a costume.
