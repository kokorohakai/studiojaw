Vendors' information

Thank you for your interest in becoming a JAFAX vendor!

How to become a vendor

E-mail vendors@jafax.org. Table space is plentiful but finite, we do sometimes have to turn away vendors if we are full. Vendors are mostly accepted on a first-come, first served basis, though we do reserve the right to select vendors who bring something particularly unique to the event.

Vendor information

  • All tables are approximately 2 1/2’ x 6’.
  • Cost for table space is $150 per table. The first $150 of the total cost may be in the form of merchandise, which will be used as prizes for contests throughout the convention
  • Payment is due the day of the event. Check or money order, please make out to JAFAX.
  • Set up can be Friday night (20 June 2008) after 6pm or Saturday morning (21 June 2008) between 7 and 9:30 am.
  • Teardown on Sunday (22 June 2008) will be between 6pm and 8pm.
  • Retailers will have control of the space between their tables and the wall. Retailers are not allowed to set up in front of or the sides of their tables. Please take care not to damage GVSU buildings or property in any way.
  • Electricity: JAFAX cannot guarantee electrical services for cash registers or any electronic devices.  Please bring battery operated equipment. 
  • Vendors are responsible for minding their tables, and JAFAX cannot in ANY way be held responsible for theft, breakage, or other loss. However, if you have a problem with suspected theft, let one of the staff members know. We will do everything we can to help resolve any issues and will, and campus police will be readily available.
  • No bootleg merchandise of any kind. If you are found to have bootlegs or knockoffs, you will be asked to remove those items. This includes bootleg DVDs, bootleg CDs (including "professional" bootlegs such as Sonmay or EverAnime releases), and unlicensed t-shirts, wallscrolls, keychains, and other merchandise.
  • All retailers are responsible for applicable state and federal tax statutes.
